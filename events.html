F.E. ORIENTATION

FE orientation is an event where we welcome the fresh faces of the year. Here, we welcome them with great avidity and introduce them to the college. We also introduce them to the college committees in RAIT where the participation of young enthusiasts is highly appreciated.


TEACHERS DAY CELEBRATION

At RAIT, we respect our teachers since they are the ones who shape our lives in so many different ways. Teachers Day is a special day for all of us where all the students get to express their gratitude towards all the faculty members.

GANESH UTSAV

Raitcha Raja is one of the most exciting events that take place in our college. SUC welcomes Bappa during the Ganapathi aagman with devotional spirit and joy. We also organise some social-welfare events like blood donation camp and meals for children during Bappa's 5 day stay.

DR. VIJAY PATIL'S BIRTHDAY

We celebrate our honourable chancellor Dr. Vijay Patil Sir's birthday with great vigour. He is a man with a golden heart and under his leadership, we have excelled in every spectrum. So, as a token of appreciation, all the committees from our college present him a greeting card followed by various performances by students in order to express their gratitude.

FRESHERS DAY

Every student who steps into college, is eager about one thing and that's the fresher's day. On this day, the freshers get to display their talents on the stage and are able to interact with their fellow mates as well as their seniors. This is the event where new friendships start flourishing.

GARBA DAY

Navratri gets everyone excited! We celebrate navratri at RAIT through garba. Dressed up in traditional outfits, everybody dances to the beautiful tunes of Gujrati music and perform garba in groups.

TIE SAREE DAY / ROSE DAY

Tie and saree day has become a tradition of every college for serval years now. On this day, everyone gets decked up and hyped up for the wonderful memories that they are going to make. This day brings several opportunities to form strong bonds with our friends and faculty. Looking beautiful in sarees and suits, the students enjoy to the fullest on this day.

TRADITIONAL DAY

Here in RAIT, we stand by our traditional values and cultures. To celebrate every tradition and culture, we celebrate traditional day where all RAITians portray their tradition through their splendid attires.

HORIZON 3-DAYS OF MADNESS

Horizon - 3 days of madness, being the biggest fest held in Navi Mumbai includes some amazing day events with lively activities and competitions like zorbing, mechanical bull, paintball etc. The night events are packed with sensational performances by renowned artists during the EDM night and Bollywood live in. Horizon's main stage music festival is the spirit of each and every RAITian.

B.E. FAREWELL

Every new beginning comes from another beginning's end. In RAIT, we give our BEs an outstanding farewell for their journey through college. With this celebration we gift everyone, memories for a lifetime.
