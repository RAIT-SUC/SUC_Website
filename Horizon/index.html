About HORIZON 2022

RAIT-SUC organizes the biggest cultural fest of Navi Mumbai-'Horizon - 3 days of madness', every year which is an amalgam of fun, learning, high-spirits, adrenaline and unforgettable moments.

This 3-day extravaganza brings out our competitive spirit through various fun day games. The night is decked with artists like Mohit Chauhan, Neha Kakkar, Harrdy Sandhu, Sachin Jigar, Benny Dayal, Nakash Aziz and many others who rock the show with incredible performances.

With our passionate staff on board too, we try and go beyond the bars we have set every year!
