ABOUT US

Established 28 years ago, the Student Union Council is the student body of Ramrao Adik Institute of Technology, Nerul. We are the organising committee behind all the cultural events that take place in the college like FE Orientation, Teacher’s Day, Vijay Patil Sir’s Birthday, Ganesh Utsav Celebration, Independence Day, Garba Celebration, Fresher’s Day, Republic Day, Traditional Day, Tie and saree day, BE Farewell and Horizon.Our committee is the voice of the students of the college and a source of an individual's overall development apart from the academics. We also overlook the anti-ragging committee and ensure the safety and comfort of the students, making their college journey, a smooth one.

MEET THE TEAM BEHIND THE SUCCESS

Our college stands for a lot of things; strength, courage, patience, perseverance, feelings and students. And with so much at its heart, it becomes important that someone preserves its essence and takes forward and higher its legacy and glory. That’s the vision our Student Union Council chases relentlessly and encourages all to follow; and this effort rests on some very bold, confident and dependable shoulders. These pillars are responsible for conducting an array of shiny events which blow life into the walls of RAIT, for gifting us countless memories and for making us feel proud about belonging to this warm family. The Core welcomes every new face to explore the culture at RAIT. You will add to us, a shade of yours, and return as a whole new colour.
